Scout Programs

  1. Home
  2. Scout Programs

Looking for something different to do with your Scout Troop? As You Wish has great options! Whether you are looking for a creative meeting idea, a Girl Scout looking to fulfill the requirements of your Potter Badge or Business Owner Badge or a Boy Scout working on your Webelos Artist Explosion badge or Arrow of Light Elective, As You Wish has something for everyone!



Brownie Potter Badge

As You Wish will work with your troop to fulfill the requirements of the Brownie Potter Badge:
Find some Pottery: We will take a tour of our studio and point out and discuss all of the pottery we use in our daily lives.
Get To Know Clay: An explanation of what clay is and how to properly store and care for it.
Make a Simple Pot: We will make a pinch pot with their clay slab*
Make an Art Piece: We will make a simple sculpture with their clay slab*
Paint and Glaze It: We will learn about underglazes and overglazes, paint a six inch tile, overglaze it, and learn about the firing process.

$12 per painter includes all supplies, glazing and firing, 6 inch tile and As You Wish souvenir patch. Minimum of 6 painters.

*Due to time constraints clay items will not be fired. Participants can take them home to dry or save the wet clay to work with at a later date.

Junior Owner Badge

Webelos Art Explosion Badge

Arrow of Light Elective

As You wish instructors will guide your troop through fulfilling the requirements of your Webelos Art Explosion Badge and/or Arrow of Light Elective including:

* Visit an art exhibit
* Create Self Portraits
* Use Clay to create a simple form
* Create an object out of clay that can be fired, baked in the oven or air dried
* Display your artwork in a pack, school or community art show


$12 per painter includes all supplies, glazing and firing, 6 inch tile and As You Wish souvenir patch. Minimum of 6 painters.

Creative Meetings
